# Placement Aptitude Research: Company Paper Analysis

## Executive Summary

After deep research into TCS NQT, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Capgemini, Cognizant, and other major company papers, here are the critical gaps in our current question bank that prevent realistic mock tests.

## TCS NQT (Most Important)

### Exam Pattern 2026
- **Foundation Round**: 65 questions, 75 minutes
  - Numerical Ability: 20 questions, 25 min
  - Verbal Ability: 25 questions, 25 min
  - Reasoning Ability: 20 questions, 25 min
- **Advanced Round**: 14-16 MCQs + 2 coding, 115 minutes
  - Advanced Quant: shared 25 min
  - Advanced Reasoning: shared 25 min
  - Advanced Coding: 90 min

## Wipro

### Pattern
- Aptitude: 16 questions, 16 min (very fast!)
- Logical Reasoning: 14 questions, 14 min
- English: 22 questions, 18 min
- Coding: 2 questions, 60 min

### Wipro Specifics
- Speed is key: ~1 min per question
- Heavy on **Data Interpretation** and **Series**
- **Game-Based Aptitude** (new): Pattern matching, switch challenge, grid challenge, motion challenge, team selection — these are completely missing from our bank

**MAJOR GAP**: Game-based aptitude (cognitive games) is completely absent.

---

## Accenture

### Pattern
- Cognitive Assessment: 50 questions, 50 min
  - English: 20-25 questions
  - Critical Thinking: 15-20 questions
  - Problem Solving: 15-20 questions
- **Cognitive games** (new since 2024): Switch challenge, grid challenge, motion challenge

### Accenture Specifics
- Heavy on **critical reasoning** and **deductive logic**
- **Switch Challenge**: Pattern matching game
- **Grid Challenge**: Memory + pattern game
- **Motion Challenge**: Tracking moving objects
- **Team Selection**: Deductive puzzle

**MAJOR GAP**: Game-based cognitive assessments and team selection puzzles.

---

## Capgemini

### Pattern
- Pseudo Code: 20 questions, 20 min
- English: 20 questions, 20 min
- Game-Based Aptitude: 4 games
- Behavioral Assessment: 100 questions, untimed
- Adaptive Test: Difficulty changes based on performance

**MAJOR GAP**: Behavioral/psychometric assessment, adaptive test logic.
