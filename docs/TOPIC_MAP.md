# Complete Topic Map for Placement Exams

## 1. Quantitative Aptitude

### Number System & Fundamentals
- **number_system**: Natural numbers, whole numbers, integers, rational/irrational numbers, prime/composite, even/odd, face value, place value
- **divisibility**: Divisibility rules (2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 11), unit digit problems, remainder theorems
- **hcf_lcm**: Highest Common Factor, Least Common Multiple, HCF-LCM relationship, product of numbers = HCF * LCM
- **factors_multiples**: Number of factors, sum of factors, perfect numbers

### Arithmetic
- **percentages**: Percentage change, successive percentages, population increase/decrease, election problems
- **profit_loss**: Cost price, selling price, marked price, discount, successive discounts, false weight
- **simple_interest**: SI formula, rate calculation, time calculation, amount calculation
- **compound_interest**: CI formula, half-yearly/quarterly compounding, difference between CI and SI
- **ratios_proportions**: Ratio comparison, compound ratio, duplicate/subduplicate ratios, partnership applications
- **averages**: Average of numbers, weighted average, average age, average speed, replacement problems
- **ages**: Age problems with ratios, future/past ages, family age problems

### Time & Work Related
- **time_work**: Work rate, men and days, pipe filling/emptying, efficiency problems
- **pipes_cisterns**: Inlet/outlet pipes, combined work, leak problems
- **time_speed_distance**: Speed conversions, average speed, relative speed, meeting points
- **trains**: Train crossing pole/platform/another train, signal problems
- **boats_streams**: Upstream, downstream, still water speed, stream speed

### Algebra & Counting
- **partnership**: Profit sharing, sleeping partner, working partner, ratio of investments
- **mixtures_alligations**: Mixing two ingredients, replacement problems, alligation rule, mean price
- **permutations_combinations**: nPr, nCr, arrangements, selections, circular permutations, derangements
- **probability**: Classical probability, dice, cards, coins, balls in bag, at least/at most problems

### Geometry & Data
- **mensuration**: Area, perimeter, volume of 2D and 3D shapes, surface area
- **clocks**: Angle between hands, coincidences, time gained/lost, mirror image time
- **calendars**: Day of the week, leap years, odd days, century calculations
- **data_interpretation**: Tables, bar charts, pie charts, line graphs, caselets, missing data

## 2. Logical Reasoning

### Pattern Based
- **series**: Number series, letter series, alpha-numeric series, pattern recognition
- **coding_decoding**: Letter shifting, number coding, word coding, substitution, matrix coding
- **analogy**: Number analogy, word analogy, letter analogy, figure analogy

### Relationship Based
- **blood_relations**: Family tree, generation gaps, relationship puzzles, coded relations
- **direction_sense**: Cardinal directions, shadow-based, distance calculation, turning problems

### Arrangement Based
- **seating_arrangement**: Linear (single/dual row), circular, rectangular, with/without constraints
- **puzzles**: Floor puzzles, scheduling, tabulation, combination puzzles
- **syllogisms**: 2-statement, 3-statement, 4-statement, Venn diagram method, possibility cases

### Logic Based
- **inequalities**: Direct inequalities, coded inequalities, either-or cases
- **statements_assumptions**: Implicit assumptions, strength of argument, inference
- **logical_deductions**: Conclusion following, course of action, cause and effect
- **data_sufficiency**: One/two/three statements sufficient to answer

## 3. Verbal Ability

### Grammar & Usage
- **grammar**: Parts of speech, tenses, voices, narration, subject-verb agreement
- **sentence_correction**: Error identification, sentence improvement, underlined portion correction
- **error_spotting**: Single error, multiple error, no error detection
- **fill_blanks**: Single blank, double blank, contextual fill, preposition/conjunction fill

### Vocabulary
- **synonyms_antonyms**: Word meaning, contextual usage, word substitution
- **idioms_phrases**: Common idioms, phrasal verbs, proverb meanings
- **sentence_completion**: Logical completion, coherence-based

### Comprehension
- **reading_comprehension**: Short passages (100-200 words), inference-based, fact-based, vocabulary in context, title identification
- **para_jumbles**: 4-6 sentence rearrangement, opening/closing sentence identification
- **cloze_test**: Passage with multiple blanks
